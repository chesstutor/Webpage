Who are we

ChessTutor comprises of 7 Computer-Oritneted students at the University of Lincoln, a percentage of us study Computer Science, while others study Computer Information Systems as well as Games Computing. We are all Chess enthusiasts and enjoy playing Chess.

History

In 2015, we had the idea of having a person being able to teach and guide Chess players through their games when several new-comers needed assistance in their game of Chess. Chess Tutor came alive in early 2016 and the amount of users rapidly increased with over 3,000 player in the first month!

Our Goal

At Chess Tutor, we like to help people - so as a community we would like to grow Chess tutor towards people that cannot play Chess. Our user-based chat system has proven very helpful to users because instead of going in blind, people can learn and play chess with help.

I've never been much of a computer guy at least in terms of playing with computers. Actually until I was about 11 I didn't use a computer for preparing for games at all. I was playing a bit online, was using the chess club mainly. Now, obviously, the computer is an important tool for me preparing for my games.
